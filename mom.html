MOM

There's nothing quite like the bond between a mother and her children. Mothers provide love, comfort, and support in a way that's truly unique. They're often the first person a child turns to for help, advice, or a hug. Mothers play a vital role in shaping their children's lives, teaching them values, and helping them become well-rounded individuals. The love and support of a mother is irreplaceable, and their role in the family is truly invaluable.

Whenever I'm looking at my mother, I feel like I'm looking at the purest love I will ever know. There is nothing as powerful as my mum’s love, and nothing as healing as her smile, happiness is seeing her smile.

There is no role in life that is more essential than that of motherhood Mothers are like glue. Even when you can’t see them, they’re still holding the family together.
All that I am, or ever hope to be, I owe to my angel mother.
